We are excited to share that on **Wednesday, 22 April 2026**, **Prof. Dan R. Ghica**, Director of the Edinburgh Programming Language Laboratory at Huawei UK, will be speaking at **Open Community Experience 2026 (OCX 2026)** in Brussels.

Together with **Jaroslaw Marek**, Huawei's Head of Open Source OS Development, Dan will present the latest developments on **Eclipse Oniro**, an open and auditable operating system, and introduce the **Cangjie Programming Language**, Huawei's new open-source programming language designed for safe, high-performance development across diverse hardware platforms.

## Event Details

- **Date:** Wednesday, 22 April 2026
- **Time:** 11:45-12:30 CET
- **Location:** Brussels
