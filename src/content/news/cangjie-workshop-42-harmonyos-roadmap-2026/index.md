# Cangjie Workshop #42: HarmonyOS Applications and the 2026 Roadmap

The first Cangjie Community Workshop (#42) of 2026 is online. In this episode, three speakers share their experiences with **Cangjie development for HarmonyOS applications** and reveal the roadmap for **Cangjie in 2026**.

## Highlights

- **MeetAI**: Award-winning AI meeting assistant built with Cangjie, featuring real-time speech transcription, intelligent summary generation, and HarmonyOS integration.
- **Smart Office**: Lightweight attendance and task management system demonstrating Cangjie's practical capabilities.
- **2026 Roadmap**: Community development workflow, LTS/STS version plans, and upcoming features including a central repository, full static linking, and the actor model.

## Featured Projects

- **MeetAI** (1st Prize, Cangjie Programming Challenge)
- **Smart Office System** (3rd Prize, Cangjie Programming Challenge)
